0. Introduction

The 7th International Blockchain Olympiad or IBCOL 2024 invites current and recent students to design comprehensive web3 solutions for real-world challenges.

0.1 Glossary

Common acronyms and terminology used in IBCOL

  • APAC. Asia-Pacific region.
  • AMER. Americas region.
  • CC. Competitions Committee. A subcommittee of the IBCOL 2024 OC.
  • EMEA. Europe, Middle East, Africa region.
  • EPC. Event Production Committee. A subcommittee of the IBCOL 2024 OC.
  • IBCOL. International Blockchain Olympiad. Annual global student competition for web3 innovation. Founded 2017.
  • IBCOL 2024. The 2024 International Blockchain Olympiad is the 7th International Blockchain Olympiad, to be held in The Netherlands in mid-November 2024.
  • OC. Organising Committee. An organisation convened to organise and deliver a blockchain olympiad at the national, regional, or international scale.
  • Preliminary. An IBCOL preliminary is a non-binding competition event that precedes the main IBCOL Finals competition event. The non-binding nature of the preliminaries provide a forum for contestants to practice as well as organising committees to shortlist or refine team and project qualities. Preliminaries may be held at a national or regional level to amplify value and impact.
  • Triumph. A triumph was a spectacular celebration held in ancient Rome for a commander and this team who had won an important victory. In a spirit of a similar fashion, IBCOL celebrates the champions of the International Blockchain Olympiad in each year in close, exclusive ceremonies, connecting them with the past via former champions and with the future, while providing a space for projects to showcase to more interested parties.

0.2 Mission

To encourage and equip the next generation of innovative and inquisitive minds to direct their creativity and passion for designing solutions for sustainable development of humanity.

0.3 Target Participants

For current students and recent students [within 3 years of stopping studies] seeking careers as solution architects, product designers, product developers, tech consulting, management consulting, and tech startup founders.

0.5 Participation Fees

To help balance the budget for producing this extraordinary event, the IBCOL 2024 Organizing Committee approves the levying of registration and administration fees. The schedule of fees are to be released at a later date. Subsidies may be applied for individuals and organizations, especially for those in need and will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL)

IBCOL is an annual web3 solution design competition for students and fresh graduates from any field of study or with plans to work in any industry. IBCOL hosts a global community of young people aspiring to find their place in the 21st century digital economy via the realm of trust technologies.
